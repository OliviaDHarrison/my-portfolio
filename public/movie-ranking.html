The Best Movies According To Olivia

Top Three Favorite Movies

Lord of the Rings Trilogy

The Lord of the Rings films are a perfect blend of breathtaking cinematography, masterfully crafted music, awe inspiring acting, magical effects, and genius directing that work together to create the epitome of epic fantasy storytelling unaffected by time.

Spirited Away

This unique story follows a girl who finds herself transported to a magical, and often frightening, world and learns to be brave in the face of adversity. The land that Jahiro navigates is colorful, lively, creative, and strange; a small village and bath house filled with creatures and spirits. Everything from the art to the music is enchanting and dreamlike. What is most remarkable about this film, is the lifelike gestures and realism of the characters. There is no detail left unturned.

The Harry Potter Series

These films are filled to the brim with magic, humor, adventure, memorable characters, fantastical creatures, and an unforgettable story. In truth, there are many stories told within Harry Potter; not just of the protagonist himself. One of the most astonishing aspects of this franchise is the multitude of alternate narratives that branch out and follow other characters; in turn providing a plethera of more lore and sustenance to the world of Harry Potter as a whole. There is no shortage of creativity here.
